How BloodLink Works

Donor Registration

Donors can register on our platform, specifying their blood type, availability, and location. The process is simple and takes just a few minutes.

Find Matching Donors

Patients and hospitals can request blood and instantly find matching donors nearby using our advanced matching algorithm.

Geolocation Services

Our geolocation technology calculates distances between donors and recipients, triggering SOS alerts in urgent cases to find the closest available help.

Instant Notifications

We send notifications via SMS, email, or push alerts to ensure that communication is immediate and effective when blood is needed.

Admin Dashboard

Our comprehensive admin dashboard allows for monitoring, verification of users, and generation of analytical reports to improve the system.

Save Lives

By connecting donors with those in need quickly and efficiently, BloodLink helps save lives and strengthen communities.
