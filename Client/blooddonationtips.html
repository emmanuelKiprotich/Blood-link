Before You Donate

  • Eat a healthy meal and stay hydrated.
  • Avoid alcohol and fatty foods 24 hours before donation.
  • Get enough sleep the night before.
  • Bring your ID or donor card.

During Donation

  • Relax and take deep breaths.
  • Wear loose, comfortable clothing.
  • Inform staff if you feel dizzy or unwell.

After Donation

  • Rest for a few minutes and drink fluids.
  • Avoid heavy lifting or strenuous exercise for 24 hours.
  • Eat foods rich in iron and vitamin C.
